]]> Who is Captain Jeffrey A. Schneider and How is He Qualified to Navigate the Murky Tax Seas?

The idea of receiving a letter from the IRS is not a pleasant one for anyone. What makes it worse is the confusion, the fear, the assumptions and the thoughts of all the worst-case scenarios that can make you feel like you have a tidal wave of stress.

Chapter 1 of Now What? The IRS is Taking Everything I Own. Help! focuses on exactly who Jeff is, and why he is great with taxpayers like yourself.

An EA is the only federally licensed tax professional. 

For over 35 years, Jeffrey Schneider EA, CTRS, NTPIF has been helping taxpayers like you:

  • File tax returns
  • Respond to IRS notices
  • Solve IRS debts

Location

His practice is located on Florida’s Treasure Coast with a satellite office in Palm Beach. Jeff represents clients all over the country… because again, an Enrolled Agent is the only federally licensed tax pro!

What do the Other Credentials Mean? 

In 2010, Jeff became a fellow of the National Tax Practice Institute. This program focuses solely on the many facets of representing taxpayers, individuals, and businesses before the IRS.

In 2015, he passed an advanced exam offered by the American Society of Tax Problem Solvers (ASTPS) to become a Certified Tax Resolution Specialist (CTRS).

Jeffrey A. Schneider EA, CTRS, NTPIF, ACT-E has earned the designation of Advanced Crypto Tax- Expert (ACT-E) from the Academy of Crypto Tax. He can help you or anyone you know (even if they are into “mining”) deal with the complicated tax implication of the Crypto World.

He recently had the honor of being selected to serve a three-year term on the Internal Revenue Service Advisory Council (IRSAC). The purpose of this council is to make recommendations to the Internal Revenue Service to ensure a fair and just tax system for all.

If anyone is equipped to navigate the murky tax seas, it’s Captain Jeffrey A. Schneider.
